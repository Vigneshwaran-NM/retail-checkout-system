Voice Commands Guide

Cart Operations

  • "Add [barcode] times [quantity]" - Add item to cart
  • "Remove [item name]" - Remove from cart
  • "Clear cart" - Empty the cart
  • "Show cart" - Read cart contents

Discount & Pricing

  • "Discount [amount] percent" - Percentage discount
  • "Discount [amount] rupees" - Flat discount
  • "Clear discount" - Remove discount
  • "Show total" - Read current total

Bill Operations

  • "Generate bill" - Create and print bill
  • "Print bill" - Print current bill
  • "Save bill" - Save without printing

Product Search

  • "Search [product name]" - Search products
  • "Top items" - Show frequently bought
  • "Price of [item]" - Get item price

Tips:

  • Speak clearly and naturally
  • Use short, direct commands
  • System will confirm each action
  • Works completely offline
  • Supports multiple languages
